insightsThe Knowledge Devaluation Crisis

According to Jesse Campbell's latest research, we're witnessing the systematic devaluation of specialized knowledge that has traditionally justified premium university education.

Key Finding: AI is collapsing the knowledge asymmetry that has historically justified high university tuition. When AI can perform complex analysis, write legal briefs, or diagnose medical conditions, the economic premium for these skills evaporates.

How AI Undermines Traditional Education Value

  • Knowledge Democratization: AI makes expert-level knowledge accessible to everyone
  • Skill Obsolescence: Technical skills become outdated faster than degree programs can adapt
  • Credential Inflation: Degrees no longer guarantee employment or high salaries
  • Performance Gap Collapse: AI narrows the gap between novices and experts
